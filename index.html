Mtg Frog

Mtg Frog is a powerful utility app for Magic: The Gathering.

All cards information you may need, whenever you want, with a clean and simple material design graphic.

Mtg Frog is designed to work offline: you can search a card by name or use the advanced search features.

Search card faster

Searching for a card is faster than light thanks to autocomplete and expansion icons!

Powerful advanced search

Mtg Frog lets you search for cards using lots of search filters.
Even offline!

Finding the right card for your deck couldn't be simpler!

Card images and prices lookup

Mtg Frog brings you the latest prices information from TCGPlayer.com and Magiccardmarket.eu.

Want to see full card? Just tap on the image.

All informations. Even offline.

All cards information are available offline. Rarity, tournament legality, oracle text, rulings and more.

Want to see another printing of the card? Just swipe left!

Lifecounter

Mtg Frog include a life counter to help you in all your Magic matches, from standard to commander!
